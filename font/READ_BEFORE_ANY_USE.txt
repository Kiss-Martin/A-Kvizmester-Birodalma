Newpitch Script is a retro script font with a smooth, flowing feel. Its slanted characters evoke the design of vintage signage and the spirit of mid-20th century sport branding.

Use underscore _ anywhere in a word to make an underline. 
Example: Super_star

Use multiple underscores to make longer swashes.
Example: Base____ball

The thick, even strokes and rounded terminals give it a soft and approachable appearance. The letters connect fluidly, creating a dynamic rhythm across words, much like a custom logo. Its elegant curves would make it suitable for athletic posters, product logos, and anything needing a friendly yet distinguished flair.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

Mans Greback designed this font with a vintage style in mind, wanting a smooth and casual feeling, creating a type that blends retro aesthetics with contemporary readability.
